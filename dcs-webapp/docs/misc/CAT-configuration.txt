7 - Configure the ContentAlignment Tool (CAT)
CAT is a service that suggests ASN (Achievement Standards Network)
Education standards that may be supported by a particular resource. It uses
natural language process techniques to process the resource content (the content
of the web resource pointed to by a the resourceURL) to determine potentially
relevant standards from the current ASN Standards Document. The
suggested standards may then be accepted or rejected by the cataloger.
